📘 About

Split-It helps you calculate and settle shared expenses quickly and fairly. Perfect for friends, roommates, or anyone sharing costs for food, travel, or events.

⚙️ How It Works

  1. Enter participant names below
  2. Click "Continue" to enter how much each person paid
  3. Click "Calculate" to see who owes whom
